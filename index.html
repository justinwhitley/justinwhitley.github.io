About
Portrait placeholder — swap for a real photo

Hi, I'm Justin.

I'm a Senior UX Designer with nearly fourteen years and eight months at Amazon, where I've spent the last several years designing for fintech and operations-focused product teams. My path here wasn't traditional — I started as a software developer, and the years I spent building the systems before I started designing them gave me a perspective most designers don't have: I know exactly what it takes to ship what I'm proposing.

My approach centers on understanding the problem before reaching for a screen. I spend as much time in stakeholder interviews, usability studies, and operational data as I do in Figma, because the best solutions for complex financial and back-office systems usually come from reframing the question rather than polishing the wrong answer. I've worked closely with engineers, product managers, and operations teams throughout my career, and that overlap is still where I do my best work.

"Good design should be invisible — and easier to build, because I've been the one building it."

Outside of work, I mentor early-career designers making the same jump from engineering into design, and I'm slowly working through every hiking trail within a few hours of Nashville. I'm currently looking for my next Senior or Staff-level role, helping a fintech or operations team build the kind of design culture that outlasts any single project.
